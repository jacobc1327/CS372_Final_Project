# Five / Three / One style programs

Templates like 5/3/1 rotate main-lift rep schemes across weeks while assistance work fills gaps in volume and weak points.

## Waves and assistance

The “wave” is the spine of the program. Assistance can flex—higher reps for size, shorter for time—without rewriting the main progression.

## Autoregulation in practice

AMRAP-style top sets are one autoregulation tool: good days add a little work, rough days stay conservative while keeping the same weekly rhythm.
