# Deloading

A deload is a short window where volume and/or intensity drop so fatigue fades while coordination and intent stay sharp.

## When to insert a deload

Rising soreness, irritability, poor sleep, or stalled reps despite “eating the program” are common signs. You do not need a perfect biomarker—if two or three signals align, a deload is reasonable.

## What to change first

Cut sets or back-off percentages before deleting whole sessions. Most athletes keep frequency and exercise order so habits and timing stay intact.
