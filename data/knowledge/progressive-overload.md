# Progressive overload

Progressive overload means asking a little more of the body over time—usually through load, reps, sets, density, or technical quality—not random chaos each session.

## Week-to-week load

Small, planned bumps beat hero weeks followed by crashes. If recovery markers (sleep, soreness, bar speed) worsen, hold load flat for a week instead of forcing the spreadsheet.

## Same structure, smarter progression

You can keep the same lifts and weekly layout while progressing accessories or secondary work first. That preserves program identity while still moving the stimulus needle.
