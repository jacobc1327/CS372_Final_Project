# Program adaptation

Adaptation means changing training while preserving what makes a program recognizable—its main lifts, weekly rhythm, and progression story.

## Global dials first

Volume percentage, average intensity, and training frequency are global levers. They shift stress without rewriting every exercise line-by-line.

## Evidence over vibes

Pair changes with simple signals: sleep hours, soreness trends, rep quality, and adherence. Retrieval-backed notes are meant to justify a dial turn, not to replace judgment.
