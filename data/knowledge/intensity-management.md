# Intensity management

Intensity here means how close sets are to failure or to a heavy working percentage, not “how loud the playlist is.”

## Heavy versus grindy

Very heavy singles and hard triples stress the nervous system differently than long sets near failure. Programs usually mix both on purpose—respect the mix when adapting.

## Raising intensity safely

Raise intensity in small steps and keep technique fixed. If bar speed collapses early, you gained little except risk—pull back and own the lighter sets.
