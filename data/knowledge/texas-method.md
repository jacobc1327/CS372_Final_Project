# Texas Method style rhythm

A classic rhythm is heavy volume early in the week, a light recovery session mid-week, and a higher-intensity day later. The structure trades session-to-session feel for weekly balance.

## Recovery between volume and intensity

The light day exists so Friday quality is not paid for with Monday’s ego. If the light day creeps heavy, the whole week’s intent drifts.

## Progress decisions

Progress when reps are crisp and bar speed holds. If Friday grinds while Monday feels awful, fix recovery and technique before adding load.
