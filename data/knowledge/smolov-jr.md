# Smolov-style microcycles

Short specialization blocks concentrate frequency and volume on one lift family. They assume you can recover between dense sessions.

## Honest readiness

If sleep is short or joints are hot, the block still “fits on paper” but may not fit you. Shaving a set or repeating a week is smarter than running the spreadsheet into the ground.

## Exit strategy

Plan what happens after the block: a lighter week or a return to balanced training so fatigue does not carry into unrelated lifts.
