# Volume management

Training volume is the product of sets, reps, and hard efforts across the week. It drives adaptation but also drives fatigue.

## Spreading versus stacking

Spreading similar work across more days can feel easier per session but raises weekly coordination cost. Stacking can work if sleep and nutrition are solid.

## Matching volume to recovery budget

If recovery is thin, reduce secondary work before main lifts. Accessories are the usual dial because they contribute fatigue without defining the program’s character.
